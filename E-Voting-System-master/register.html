Instructions for Registration -

  1. For the registration as a voter, you need an Aadhaar card.
  2. The image of the QR code on the Aadhaar card is to be uploaded for registration.
  3. The QR code is a black and white colored square shape image on your Aadhaar card.
  4. The Aadhaar ID or UID will be your unique ID for logging in and voting.
  5. To proceed to the registration, click "Register Now" below.
